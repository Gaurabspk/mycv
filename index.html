About Me

Hello! I'm Gaurab, a passionate and experienced travel guide specializing in Nepalese adventures. With over 8 years of experience in the tourism industry, I have guided hundreds of travelers through the majestic Himalayas and cultural treasures of Nepal.

My expertise includes trekking routes at all difficulty levels, cultural tours, wildlife safaris, and spiritual journeys. I'm fluent in English, Hindi, and Nepali, and have a deep understanding of local customs and traditions.

I take pride in creating personalized itineraries that match each traveler's interests and fitness levels. Whether you're seeking an epic Everest Base Camp trek, a peaceful retreat in Pokhara, or a cultural immersion in Kathmandu Valley, I'll ensure you have an authentic and unforgettable experience.

My mission is to help travelers from all over the world discover the beauty, culture, and adventure that Nepal has to offer while ensuring their safety and comfort throughout the journey.

Destinations & Services

Everest Base Camp

The ultimate trekking adventure to the base of the world's highest mountain. Experience Sherpa culture, breathtaking views, and a sense of achievement.

Annapurna Circuit

One of the world's classic treks circling the Annapurna Massif, featuring diverse landscapes from subtropical forests to high-altitude deserts.

Annapurna Base Camp

A shorter but spectacular trek that takes you into the heart of the Annapurna Sanctuary surrounded by towering peaks.

Langtang Valley

Known as the "valley of glaciers," this trek offers stunning mountain scenery and a chance to experience Tamang culture.

Manaslu Circuit

A challenging and less crowded trek around the eighth highest mountain in the world, offering pristine landscapes and rich cultural encounters.

Upper Mustang

Explore the ancient Kingdom of Lo, a remote region with Tibetan-influenced culture, cave dwellings, and a dramatic desert landscape.

Pokhara Tour

Discover Nepal's adventure capital with its serene lakes, stunning mountain views, and vibrant nightlife.

Kathmandu Tour

Immerse yourself in the cultural heart of Nepal with visits to ancient temples, palaces, and bustling markets.

Chitwan Safari

Experience jungle safaris in Chitwan National Park, home to rhinos, tigers, elephants, and diverse birdlife.

Lumbini Pilgrimage

Visit the birthplace of Lord Buddha and explore monasteries from different Buddhist traditions around the world.
